Roll the Dice

Two days of games, stories & unforgettable moments

From Blood on the Clocktower to Tabletop RPGs, from community puzzles to murder mysteries - REPLAY brings together Bangalore's board gaming community for a weekend of play, discovery, and connection.

Whether you're a seasoned strategist or rolling your first dice, REPLAY has something for everyone. Jump into intense sessions of BOTC, lose yourself in a TTRPG adventure, crack a community puzzle, or discover your next favourite game from our all-day board game library.

Plus, catch the exclusive pre-launch of Cats from Hell, grab deals during Store Price Drop Hour, and close out the evenings with a Murder Mystery night and a Quiz showdown.
